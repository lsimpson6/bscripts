OUR WORK IN THE U.S.

28,405

vulnerable kids & families served in the U.S.

Your partnership is changing the future of family. Across the nation, family strengthening and preservation services are keeping families together through some of the toughest challenges. Because of you, children are safer and their families are stronger. In 2023, more children than ever found safety, love, and connection through family. And for a child, those things can change the world.

Wins & Highlights

Wins & Highlights

40% GROWTH

Safe Families for Children supported 480 children and 355 families with more than 1,700 services—a growth of 40% over last year!

152 FAMILIES

Parent Cafés are growing! Seven active groups throughout the country provided 152 families with a safe place to access resources, learn parenting skills, and meet peers who can offer social support.

867 FAMILIES

HOMEBUILDERS® expanded from five to 13 locations across six states, helping strengthen 867 families. A growth of 227% from last year!

137 MOMS

ReNew helped 137 moms heal and recover from substance use and prepare to safely parent their newborns.

5,100 CHILDREN

Provided more than 5,100 children in foster care with a safe, loving place to stay by licensing 198 new foster families and retaining 72% of current foster families.

Wins & Highlights

Wins & Highlights

1,814 REFUGEES

Adding three new reception and placement sites allowed us to welcome 1,814 refugees into their new communities—a 50% increase from last year!

EXPANDED SERVICES

Services were expanded for unaccompanied minors, giving more children in the U.S. refuge, safety, shelter, and education.

5,000+ CHILDREN

Nearly 1,000 unaccompanied minors and their families prepared for reunification. More than 5,000 children and their families were supported after reunification, a 66% growth from last year!

2,600+ CHILDREN

More than 2,600 unaccompanied minors were given a safe place to stay through foster care, a 37% growth in services from last year!

50+ AGENCIES

In Northern California, we’re leading the effort to protect unaccompanied minors, hosting monthly educational meetings for more than 50 other agencies across the state.

OUR WORK AROUND THE WORLD

8,632

global children, youth, and families served

Across the globe, children and families are trying to stay together despite the forces pulling them apart. Your generosity is helping. Together, we’ve protected children and empowered youth in Colombia and reunified and strengthened families in Ethiopia and Ghana. Thank you for giving children and youth the opportunity to grow and thrive, surrounded by the love and safety of their families and communities.

Wins & Highlights

Wins & Highlights

CHILD RIGHTS

A child’s right to a family is now law in Ethiopia! The Directive on Alternative Care and Support ensures quality care is provided for children within families and communities, not institutions.

FAMLY CARE

The AHOPE orphanage, and others in Ethiopia and Ghana, transitioned children from institutional care to community and family-based care—where they belong and thrive.

7,127 REFUGEES AND MIRANTS

The needs in Colombia shifted, and so did we, successfully providing child protection services, youth resilience, and wraparound family care to migrants, refugees, and others who’ve been forced from their homes across Colombia.

8 PARENT GROUPS

Eight parent support groups launched in Ghana and Colombia, offering skills, knowledge, and connections parents need to provide the best care to their children.

PRAY WITH US

Refugee families

As the refugee crisis around the world worsens, pray for the children and families who’ve been forced from their homes. Pray for their safety as they wait in refugee camps. Pray they will soon arrive in their new homes and communities and neighbors will welcome them with love and understanding. Pray our capacity and funding will increase so we can help more families find home again with quality and compassionate services.

Families in crisis

As the refugee crisis around the world worsens, pray for the children and families who’ve been forced from their homes. Pray for their safety as they wait in refugee camps. Pray they will soon arrive in their new homes and communities and neighbors will welcome them with love and understanding. Pray our capacity and funding will increase so we can help more families find home again with quality and compassionate services.

Restoration for those battling addiction

The opioid epidemic is one of the most destructive crises of our time. Pray for all those battling addiction, especially parents. Pray God will place his healing hand on them and they will find hope, restoration, and peace. Pray they will find compassionate, effective support to become the parents they want to be and their children need them to be. Pray our recovery centers and programs will grow and receive financial support, so no one who needs help is ever turned away because of cost.

Children in foster care and those waiting for adoption

The needs of children in foster care grow increasingly challenging. Too many children are struggling with past trauma and intense emotional, behavioral, and mental health needs. Please pray for additional funding and support so we can meet them where they are, with accessible services that bring them healing and hope. Pray more foster families will step forward with the ability to offer the tender care these children need. Pray children in care will know the love of Jesus, feel supported, and have the resources they need to heal, grow, and see their potential is greater than their pain.

Our team

Our dedicated caseworkers and frontline staff willingly step into hard situations every day. They do it for children and families who need them. Pray the Lord will continue to kindle their passion and energy and protect their spirits from the difficult things they see. Pray our open positions will be filled with the right individuals who have a heart for children and families and are ready to serve. Please pray the Lord will bless all who are acting as his hands and feet, navigating complicated situations, and doing the emotionally challenging work that is shaping and changing lives.

Gratitude

The work we’re doing together is hard. But it’s urgent, essential, and worthwhile. Together, we are changing the world for children and families. We thank God for allowing us to be his partner in building up families and setting up generations to thrive. This past year, we’ve touched thousands of lives. And we couldn’t have done it without our faith in God and your faith in us. Please join us in thanking God for the purpose he’s laid before us and the good we’ve accomplished together.
